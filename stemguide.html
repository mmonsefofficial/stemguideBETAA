The Egyptian STEM System

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) high schools in Egypt are designed for top-performing middle school graduates. The curriculum is uniquely project-based, emphasizing critical thinking, scientific research, and real-world problem-solving rather than rote memorization.

Admission Constraints

  • Achieve a near-perfect score (usually 98%+) in the preparatory certificate.
  • Obtain full marks in at least one or two of the core subjects: English, Science, or Math.
  • Pass the rigorous multi-disciplinary STEM admission test (IQ, Math, Science, and English).
